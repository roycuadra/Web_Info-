Solar Technology Comparisons

Monocrystalline vs Polycrystalline Solar Panels

  • Monocrystalline: Made from a single crystal structure, offering higher efficiency and a sleek black appearance.
  • Polycrystalline: Made from multiple crystal fragments, slightly lower efficiency but more affordable, with a blue tint.

SCC PWM vs SCC MPPT

  • PWM (Pulse Width Modulation): Simpler technology, best for smaller systems, lower cost, but less efficient in energy conversion.
  • MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking): More advanced, maximizes power extraction from solar panels, ideal for larger systems with higher efficiency.
